How to Play

1. Click on any game title from the list to launch the emulator.

2. The controls will be shown on the emulator screen.

3. For mobile devices, touch controls will appear automatically.

4. You can save your progress using the save state feature in the emulator.

5. Game saves are stored locally in your browser.

Note: These ROMs are provided for educational and preservation purposes only. We encourage supporting the original developers by purchasing their games when available.
